Let Us Host Your Reception!

Reception and Special Event Services

At Temple Inn and Suites, we are more than just a venue for your special occasion. We provide a host of services to make your event an outstanding success. We approach each celebration as a unique opportunity to lend our knowledge, expertise and resources to you. Oftentimes, we find people who come to us are both excited and stressed. We want you to keep feeling the thrill of having your event with us, while we do all we can to relieve you of the worry.

Whether you need us to help with a little or a lot, we are here to make your day perfect. Each booking is as unique and individualized as the people we meet. We don't believe in a one-size-fits-all solution to anything we do.

We can provide wedding consultation and planning, access to wedding supplies and decorations, wedding favors, assist you in setting up a photo booth and we have the contacts to help find the perfect caterer, DJ, photographer, or videographer. Here are just a few of the stress-releiving services we provide:

Consultation -

You have thought of almost everything, but just want to take a moment and review to see what you may have missed. We can provide advice and thoughts on how to make sure nothing was forgotten.

Planning -

We sit down and discuss your plans, your budget and your dreams and do everything we can to make it come to life. We will coordinate with all the vendors needed, help organize your guest-list, conduct the rehearsal and dinner and then make sure your wedding day goes as planned. We do all the worrying, allowing you to enjoy your celebration stress and worry free!

DJ | Entertainment Referral -

Special events and wedding coordination is our business. We have an extensive list of contacts and vendors available to help keep your audience entertained. Whether looking for a DJ, a live band or any other entertainment, let us know what you are looking for and we will do all we can to make it happen.

Photographer + Videographer Referral -

Whether it's for a corporate retreat, an annual meeting, a reunion, birthday, wedding or any other special occasion, you may want to be able to go back and relive it through still and video images. If you need help locking down a professional photographer and/or videographer, we can assist you. With our long list of vendor partners and business relationships, let us find the right resource for you.

Catering Referral -

Wedding and event coordination is our passion. We have an extensive list of contacts and vendor relationships at the tips of our fingers. If you are needing a special meal for your wedding or event, bring us your requirements and we can help find the perfect caterer for you.

Supplies and Decorations -

We can help to make sure your wedding and reception are decorated just how you imagined. Tell us what you want and we will make it happen. If we don't have something in our inventory, we have relationships with many other vendors who can be brought in to help. Once we have locked down your decorating plans, we will take care of setting them up for you.

Wedding Day Coordination -

You've already planned every moment, invited family and friends and locked down all the vendors you wanted. Now it's time to relax and enjoy every second of your special day. Let us take care of the coordination of all elements of your special day. Let us do the worrying and the rounding up of family, guests, and vendors.
